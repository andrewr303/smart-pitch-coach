## 📖 About

**PitchCoach AI** is an AI-powered presentation coaching tool that helps speakers deliver confident, polished pitches. Simply upload your slide deck (PDF or PPTX), and PitchCoach AI will generate intelligent talking points, transition cues, and personalized feedback to elevate your delivery.

🔗 **Live App:** [https://guide-my-pitch.lovable.app](https://guide-my-pitch.lovable.app)

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Guides
Get **smart talking points generated for every slide** in your presentation. No more memorizing scripts — let AI craft natural, compelling speaker notes tailored to your content.

### 🎯 Never Miss a Beat
Enjoy **smooth transitions and key emphasis points** between slides. PitchCoach AI identifies the critical moments in your presentation and ensures you hit every one.
